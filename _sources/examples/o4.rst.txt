O\ :sub:`4` System
==================

The **O₄** system is a four-body arrangement of four indistinguishable
oxygen atoms colliding as two diatoms. Input files live under
``input/O4/``.

Arrangement
-----------

- Atoms: **O, O, O, O** (all identical).
- Collision entry: O\ :sub:`2` + O\ :sub:`2`.

Kinetic processes
-----------------

- **Inelastic scattering** — V–V, V–R, and V–T energy transfer between
  the two colliding O\ :sub:`2` molecules with no atom exchange.
- **Reactive exchange** — four-atom identical-atom swap; summed to a
  single exchange channel in indistinguishable mode.
- **Single dissociation** — :math:`\mathrm{O}_{2} + \mathrm{O}_{2} \to
  \mathrm{O}_{2} + 2\,\mathrm{O}`.
- **Full atomisation** — :math:`\to 4\,\mathrm{O}`.
- **Van-der-Waals O\ :sub:`4` complex formation** — long-lived
  four-atom intermediates can form on deep-well surfaces, affecting
  exchange rates; inspect the final-time distribution for long-lived
  events (see :doc:`/theory/arrangement_classification`).

PES models
----------

Available: ``UMN`` — University of Minnesota O\ :sub:`4` surface
(same functional framework as the O\ :sub:`3` / O\ :sub:`18`\
:sub:`,16` systems).

Diatomic partner: ``O2_UMN`` (matching the multi-body PES).

Typical use
-----------

O\ :sub:`2` + O\ :sub:`2` provides the four-atom closure of the
oxygen-chemistry dataset — pair it with O\ :sub:`3` results for the
full O\ :sub:`2` dissociation / recombination picture at a given
temperature. It also enables V–V rate coefficients needed for O\
:sub:`2` vibrational-ladder master-equation studies in hypersonic
aerothermodynamics.
