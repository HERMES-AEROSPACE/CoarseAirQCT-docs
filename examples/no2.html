NO2 System

The NO₂ system is a three-body arrangement with one nitrogen and two indistinguishable oxygens. Input files live under input/NO2/.

Arrangement

  • Atoms: N, O, O (the two O’s are identical).

  • Collision entries: NO + O or O2 + N.

Kinetic processes

  • Inelastic scattering on the incoming arrangement.

  • Reactive exchange between the two distinct arrangements:

    \[\mathrm{NO} + \mathrm{O} \;\leftrightarrow\; \mathrm{O}_{2} + \mathrm{N}.\]

    This is the second Zel’dovich reaction, complementing the N2O pathway (N2O System).

  • Dissociation — full atomisation \(\to\) N + O + O.

Multi-surface structure

NO2 has multiple low-lying doublet and quartet electronic surfaces:

  • \({}^{2}A'\) (UMN_2Ap)

  • \({}^{4}A'\) (UMN_4Ap)

plus the corresponding \({}^{2,4}A''\) components. Multi-surface runs are degeneracy-weighted in the adiabatic pipeline.

Correlation diagram

PES models

Available: UMN (with 2A’ and 4A’ surface labels), Basel.

Diatomic partners: NO_UMN_ForNO2_Doub, NO_UMN_ForNO2_Quart, O2_UMN (or Basel equivalents).

Input file walkthrough

From input/NO2/UMN/NO+O/input/CoarseAIR.inp. One nitrogen, two indistinguishable oxygens, two arrangements (NO + O and O2 + N), doublet and quartet electronic surfaces:

###########################################################################################
######                                                                               ######
######                         Input File for QCT Code                               ######
######                                                                               ######
###########################################################################################

   Generating Energy Levels?         = no
   Preprocessing Energy Levels List? = yes
   Running Trajectories?             = yes
   Postprocessing Trajectories?      = no

###########################################################################################

   System = NO2

   Nb of Species = 2
     Species 1 = NO       ! target
     Species 2 = O        ! projectile

   Nb of Molecules = 2
     Molecule 1 = NO
     Molecule 2 = O2

   Nb of Atoms = 3
     Atom 1 = N
       Mass [a.u.] of Atom 1 = 25526.04298d0
     Atom 2 = O
       Mass [a.u.] of Atom 2 = 29148.94559d0
     Atom 3 = O
       Mass [a.u.] of Atom 3 = 29148.94559d0

   Database Path = /path/to/coarseair/dtb/

   Relative Translational Energy Model = Boltzmann
   Nb of Translational Temperatures    = 1
     Translational Temperature [K] 1 = 10000.d0

###########################################################################################
#  RUNNING TRAJECTORIES and ANALYZING TRAJECTORIES
###########################################################################################

   ! Doublet + quartet PES run concurrently with spin-degeneracy weights
   Nb of PESs = 2
     Model, PES 1      = UMN_2Ap    ! 2 A'  doublet  (spin mult = 2)
     Degeneracy, PES 1 = 2.0
     Model, PES 2      = UMN_4Ap    ! 4 A'  quartet  (spin mult = 4)
     Degeneracy, PES 2 = 4.0

   Diatomic Potential Model, Molecule 1 = UMN_ForNO2_Doub   ! NO
   Diatomic Potential Model, Molecule 2 = UMN               ! O2

   Initial Impact Parameter Model = Stratified

The two O atoms are indistinguishable, so the two NO+O permutations are summed at post-processing via Identical Diatoms? = yes. The central reactive channel NO + O \(\leftrightarrow\) O2 + N is the second Zel’dovich reaction, complementary to the N2O pathway in N2O System.

Typical use

Together with N2O, the NO2 channel closes the “extended Zel’dovich” cycle for NO production in high-temperature air kinetics.
